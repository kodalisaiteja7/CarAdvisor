About CarAdvisr

What is a Used Car
Risk Report?

Every year, millions of used cars are sold in the U.S. — and many of them have hidden safety issues that dealers won't tell you about. CarAdvisr pulls real government safety data from the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA) and cross-references it with recalls, investigations, and manufacturer bulletins to build a complete risk profile for any vehicle.

What does the report show?

Unlike a Carfax report that shows what already happened to a specific car, our risk report shows what is likely to go wrong based on data from every owner who has ever reported a problem with that make, model, and year. We analyze complaint patterns at your exact mileage to give you a prediction — not just a history.

Who is this for?

Whether you're a first-time buyer who doesn't know what to look for, a parent searching for a safe car for your teen, or a budget shopper comparing 3 options on Facebook Marketplace — CarAdvisr gives you the knowledge you need in 30 seconds. No signup. No credit card. No catch.
